Position Overview
We are seeking a Sr. Data Engineer to lead Kiewit's technical integration with the Palantir platform, serving as the primary client-side engineering counterpart to Palantir's Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs). This role combines full-stack data engineering, front-end application development, and technical team leadership—ensuring Palantir-built workflows are integrated into Kiewit's data ecosystem and operationalized for long-term sustainability.

Kiewit is one of North America's largest and most respected construction and engineering organizations. We are building the Kiewit Intelligence Platform (KIP) to transform how we estimate, plan, and execute billion-dollar projects. Palantir Foundry and AIP are strategic components of this platform, and this role will lead the engineering effort to maximize value from that investment.

You will be the technical bridge between Palantir FDEs and Kiewit's Data Engineering and Data Science teams—ensuring that solutions built in Foundry integrate cleanly with KIP, that Kiewit staff can maintain and extend Palantir workflows, and that we build rather than buy our way into platform lock-in.

Responsibilities

Palantir Integration & Technical Leadership:
•	Serve as primary technical counterpart to Palantir FDEs for all integration work
•	Lead client-side engineering team (3-5 engineers) supporting Palantir developments
•	Translate Palantir-built ontologies and workflows into sustainable, Kiewit-maintained capabilities
•	Architect data flows between Palantir Foundry and KIP (Azure Data Lake, Snowflake, Iceberg tables)
•	Ensure Palantir data assets integrate into Kiewit's lineage tracking, data catalog, and governance frameworks
•	Evaluate build-vs-buy decisions and ensure Kiewit retains optionality in platform choices

Full-Stack Data Engineering:
•	Build and optimize data pipelines feeding Palantir Foundry from SAP, InEight, Primavera, and custom systems
•	Develop PySpark/Spark transformations within Foundry Pipeline Builder and Code Workbook
•	Design and implement data models in Palantir Ontology aligned with Kiewit's master data standards
•	Ensure data quality, validation, and monitoring for all Palantir-integrated pipelines
•	Partner with Data Engineering team to maintain bi-directional data flows between Foundry and KIP

Front-End Application Development:
•	Build and extend Palantir applications using TypeScript, React, and the Ontology SDK (OSDK)
•	Develop custom UI components for construction workflows (estimating, scheduling, procurement)
•	Integrate Palantir AIP capabilities (AI agents, LLM-powered features) into production applications
•	Create data visualizations and dashboards within Foundry Workshop and Quiver
•	Ensure applications meet Kiewit UX standards and are accessible to field and office users

Team Leadership & Knowledge Transfer:
•	Lead and mentor Kiewit engineers working alongside Palantir FDEs
•	Establish best practices for Palantir development, testing, and deployment within Kiewit
•	Document integrations, workflows, and runbooks to ensure Kiewit can operate independently
•	Drive knowledge transfer so Kiewit staff—not Palantir—own the models and business logic
•	Present technical progress and integration roadmaps to leadership

Qualifications

Required:
•	Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Data Science, or related field
•	7+ years of experience in data engineering, software development, or platform integration
•	3+ years of experience leading technical teams or cross-functional engineering efforts
•	Strong proficiency in Python, SQL, and PySpark/Spark
•	Production experience with TypeScript and React (or similar modern front-end frameworks)
•	Experience integrating enterprise data platforms with third-party SaaS or PaaS solutions
•	Background in ETL/ELT pipelines, data modeling, and data quality frameworks
•	Excellent communication skills—able to translate between vendor engineers and internal teams
•	Self-directed with ability to drive ambiguous projects to completion

Preferred:
•	Direct experience with Palantir Foundry, AIP, or Ontology development
•	Experience working alongside Forward Deployed Engineers or similar vendor integration models
•	Familiarity with construction, engineering, or manufacturing data domains
•	Experience with Azure cloud services (Data Lake, AKS, Functions, Data Factory)
•	Background in Snowflake, Iceberg/Delta Lake, or modern lakehouse architectures
•	Knowledge of data governance, lineage tracking, and metadata management (Alation, Collibra)
•	Experience with orchestration platforms (Dagster, Airflow, Prefect)
•	Exposure to LLMs, RAG patterns, and AI agent development

Technical Environment:
•	Palantir: Foundry, AIP, Ontology SDK, Pipeline Builder, Code Workbook, Workshop
•	Data: Azure Data Lake, Snowflake, Iceberg tables, Polaris catalog
•	Languages: Python, SQL, PySpark, TypeScript, React
•	Infrastructure: Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform, Azure DevOps
•	Source Systems: SAP, InEight, Primavera P6, engineering models (BIM/CAD)

What Sets This Role Apart:
•	Strategic influence: Shape how Kiewit integrates a $15M-$30M platform investment
•	Technical breadth: Full stack from data pipelines to production UI
•	Leadership opportunity: Build and lead the client-side integration team
•	Platform impact: Ensure Palantir delivers value without creating unsustainable lock-in
•	Scale: 17,000+ employees, projects across North America, $25B+ revenue trajectory
