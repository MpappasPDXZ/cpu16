Kiewit Data Services is building the next generation of AI-powered tools that will transform how we estimate, plan, and execute billion-dollar projects. This is not research - this is production AI that will directly impact project outcomes.

You will be the technical leader bridging our Data Science, MLOps, and Data Engineering teams to deliver AI capabilities where the business needs them most. If you want to build AI systems that matter at massive scale, this is the role.
